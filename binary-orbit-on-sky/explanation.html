Projection of a binary orbit on the sky plane

Interactive illustration of the meaning of the orbital elements inclination i, longitude of the ascending node Ω, and the argument of periapsis ω. Use the GUI to change the orbital elements and choose whether or not to animate the body following this orbit. You can move the GUI around with the mouse.

The grey plane is the sky plane. The axes pointing North and East are indicated. The third axis points toward the observer.
